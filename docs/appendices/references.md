# References

This section provides a comprehensive list of all sources cited throughout the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book," formatted according to APA 7th edition guidelines. This list includes academic papers, technical documentation, books, and other relevant materials that informed the content of this work. A minimum of 15 sources, with at least 50% peer-reviewed academic articles, have been integrated to ensure the scientific rigor and credibility of the material presented.
